Magda Hamilton is an American Creative Technologist and Graphics & Design Engineer based in New York. She is the founder of Velvet Disruption, a creative technology studio dedicated to building software embodying meaning.

With a background spanning software engineering, literature, and design, she works at the intersection of computation and artistic expression.

Educated at Harvard University and the Sorbonne, she has served as a Delegate to the UN Commission on the Status of Women (CSW69 and CSW70), nominated by UN Women UK, participating in panels on women in STEM, AI governance, and gender equity in the digital age.
